Transfer to Brokerage
Destination is fixed for this workflow.
Tracks holding currency context on the destination brokerage lot.
ESPP allows partial FIFO transfer using whole shares only. RSU/ESPP+ require full lot transfer.
Transfer Rules Transfer rules:
ESPP transfers keep original lot pricing, must use whole shares, and follow FIFO from the oldest active ESPP lot.
ESPP partial transfer splits custody (ESPP remainder + BROKERAGE lot). Final remainder transfer merges into the same source-linked broker lot.
ESPP+ transfers can forfeit still-locked matched shares and make employment tax eligible at transfer time.
RSU transfers are allowed only after vest.
Transfers into ISA are not supported. Use dispose then Add Lot in ISA.
