Glossary

Plain-English definitions of key terms used across this app.

Gains and Cost Terms

True Cost What you effectively paid after accounting for tax events at acquisition: income tax at vest for RSU; employer subsidy for ESPP+; purchase price for ESPP/BROKERAGE/ISA. Used in Economic Gain.
Cost Basis The HMRC-recognised cost base for calculating capital gain on disposal (also called Allowable Cost). May differ from True Cost when acquisition-time tax creates a gap.
Allowable Cost HMRC term for Cost Basis in CGT self-assessment filing context. Used in the CGT Report.
Economic Gain Proceeds minus True Cost. Shows true investment return. May differ from CGT Gain because True Cost and Cost Basis can differ.
Unrealised P&L Paper gain or loss on shares you still hold. Becomes realised only when you sell.

Tax Terms

Employment Tax Income Tax (IT) + National Insurance (NI) + Student Loan (SL) on scheme-eligible disposals (RSU, ESPP+). Abbreviated "IT + NI + SL".
AEA Annual Exempt Amount — the CGT tax-free threshold for the year (£3,000 for 2024–25 onwards). Gains below AEA in a tax year are not subject to CGT.
ANI (Adjusted Net Income) Gross Employment Income minus Pension Sacrifice plus Other Income. Determines Personal Allowance taper above £100k. Used in the Tax Plan what-if analysis.
SL (Student Loan) Student Loan repayment. Deducted alongside Income Tax and NI when employment tax is estimated on eligible scheme disposals.

Liquidity and Status Terms

Est. Net Liquidity (Sellable) Sum of Net If Sold Today for sellable rows only. Locked and forfeiture-restricted lots are excluded. Shown on the Portfolio page.
Hypothetical Full Liquidation Gross market value minus estimated employment tax across all lots (including locked and forfeiture-restricted). Shown on the Net Value page. Not directly comparable to Est. Net Liquidity.
Blocked/Restricted Value Market value of locked lots (e.g. pre-vest RSU) plus ESPP+ matched-share forfeiture-at-risk value. Cannot be realised until the lock or forfeiture window expires.
Forfeiture Window The period after ESPP+ lot acquisition during which selling the paired employee shares forfeits the matched (employer) shares. Badge "(Xd left)" means X days remaining until the window closes and it is safe to sell.
